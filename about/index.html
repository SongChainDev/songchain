Zack Gottesman Founder and CTO
Zack Gottesman Founder and CTO Zack is our CTO. He is excited to combine his passions for music and engineering to help lead a team of talented developers to build SongChain. Ethan Eason VP of Engineering
Ethan Eason VP of Engineering Ethan serves as VP of Engineering working closely with Zack, our CTO, and Sunint, our PM, to create and augment our product’s implementation plans.
